
NationalSurveyData.csv
  http://www.bgs.ac.uk/arsenic/bangladesh/Data/NationalSurveyData.csv
  The results of the survey of 3534 boreholes from 61 of the 64

MandariSurveyData.csv
  http://www.bgs.ac.uk/arsenic/bangladesh/Data/MandariSurveyData.csv
  The results of the 236 boreholes from Mandari village in Lakshmipur
  district, SE Bangladesh.

SpecialStudyData.csv
  http://www.bgs.ac.uk/arsenic/bangladesh/Data/SpecialStudyData.csv
  The results of the 271 boreholes and dug wells from the three
  Special Study Areas (Chapai Nawabganj, Faridpur and Lakshmipur).
